justify-items - This property is used to align all grid items within their grid cells along the inline axis. It applies to the entire grid container.
justify-self - This property is used to align individual grid items with their respective grid cells along the inline axis. It overrides the justify-items property for the specific grid item.
In this example, all grid items will be horizontally centered within their grid cells due to 'justify-items: center;', but the second grid item will be alligned to the start of its cell horizontally because of 'justify-self: start;'.
